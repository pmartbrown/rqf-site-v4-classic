FAQ

Questions, answered

A same-day double close: you buy the property (A→B), then sell to your end buyer (B→C) the same day. We fund the A→B purchase so you don't use your own cash. When your buyer closes, our funds are repaid instantly.

When a seller's carry-back note covers the buyer's down payment, the title company still needs real cash in escrow to close. We put up that cash. After closing, the seller-carry second note is issued and repays us plus our fee.

We front the end buyer's down payment — but instead of being repaid from a seller-carry note (as in a Stack), we're repaid directly out of the seller's proceeds, usually the wholesaler's assignment fee.

On transactional deals (EMD, Double Close, Stack, Echo), we respond within one hour. Funding follows quickly after documents and title confirmation — often same-day.

Double Close: well over $100M. EMD: up to $500K. Stack: up to $10M. Echo: up to $10M.

EMD: 5% upfront + 20% at close, or 10% upfront and nothing at close. Double Close: 1.25% up to $1M. Stack & Echo: a flat 2.5%. Plus a percentage of every referred deal that closes.
